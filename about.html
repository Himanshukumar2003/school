About Us

Adhyayan Inclusive Learning Centre

Adhyayan is a multidisciplinary special education center offering therapeutic services for children with developmental challenges like Autism, Dyslexia, ADHD, and more. We provide individualized and group therapy to support holistic development, focusing on academics, social skills, and emotional well-being in a nurturing and playful environment.

  • Comprehensive Therapeutic Services
  • Individualized & Group Sessions
  • Supportive & Nurturing Environment
  • Play-Based Learning

OUR VISION

Each child is precious and each child should get equal rights and opportunities in all spheres of life. We at Adhyayan are doing our bit by helping children with special needs in enhancing their cognitive, social & emotional needs.

OUR MISSION

Each child is precious and each child should get equal rights and opportunities in all spheres of life. We at Adhyayan are doing our bit by helping children with special needs in enhancing their cognitive, social & emotional needs.

OUR APPROACH

Ailc has been working dedicatedly for children with special needs from past 30 years and our mission is to include all children with special needs into mainstream. We strongly believe in having an INCLUSIVE Society where all CHILDREN have equal rights and equal opportunities.

OUR COMMITMENT

To deliver the best services and effective outcomes with utmost dedication. Our commitment is to help a child in successfully leading and independent life, by providing time-tested therapies in the latest restricted environment and individual education programme.

Look What Our Students Achieve

Our student BAWA ADITYA was awarded back then in 1996 by then Prime Minister Shri I. K.GUJRAL for his academic achievement as a Dyslexic student. Presently, he is successfully running a hotel business in Goa.

Our another student SHUBHANG RATHI of DPS VASANT KUNJ again made history by gaining the highest marks and his story was covered by all leading Newspapers.

Our another student ANANDINI MALHOTRA who was severely Dyslexic and a Cancer survivor became a role model for millions of differently able children and awarded by HRD Ministry in 2016.

Another student TANUJ TEWATIA from DPS Faridabad sector 19, a boy from a rural background and a severe Dyslexic became one of the toppers in 10th class board exam 2019.

It has been more than two decades that we have been working relentlessly for our CHILDREN WITH DYSLEXIA and Other Disabilities with a well supported expert team consisting of special educators, Occupational therapists, Speech therapists, Behavior therapists, Counselling Psychologists, and Career Counsellors.
