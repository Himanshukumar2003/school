Look What Our Students Achieve

Our student BAWA ADITYA was awarded back then in 1996 by then Prime Minister Shri I. K.GUJRAL for his academic achievement as a Dyslexic student. Presently, he is successfully running a hotel business in Goa.

Our another student SHUBHANG RATHI of DPS VASANT KUNJ again made history by gaining the highest marks and his story was covered by all leading Newspapers.

Our another student ANANDINI MALHOTRA who was severely Dyslexic and a Cancer survivor became a role model for millions of differently able children and awarded by HRD Ministry in 2016.

Another student TANUJ TEWATIA from DPS Faridabad sector 19, a boy from a rural background and a severe Dyslexic became one of the toppers in 10th class board exam 2019.

It has been more than two decades that we have been working relentlessly for our CHILDREN WITH DYSLEXIA and Other Disabilities with a well supported expert team consisting of special educators, Occupational therapists, Speech therapists, Behavior therapists, Counselling Psychologists, and Career Counsellors.
