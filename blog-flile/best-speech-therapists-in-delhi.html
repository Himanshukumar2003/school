Understanding Speech Therapy

Speech therapy is a specialized field dedicated to diagnosing and treating communication disorders. These disorders can range from articulation difficulties and stuttering to language delays and voice disorders. The goal of speech therapy is to enhance communication skills, promote confidence, and improve the overall quality of life.
