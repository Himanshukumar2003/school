Meet Mr. Rajeev Bhatt

Mr. Rajeev Bhatt is a distinguished psychologist and esteemed expert in the field of learning disabilities with over 20 years of experience in mental health and education. He is a renowned international speaker, a Rotary Club awardee, and a prolific columnist for The Times of India, where he shares his insights on inclusive learning. His remarkable contributions have left an indelible mark on the realm of education.

Having trained more than 5000 children and 12000 educators, Mr. Rajeev Bhatt has tirelessly worked to bridge the gap between individuals with learning disabilities and the opportunities they deserve. His dedication is further highlighted by his authorship of the book "Decoding Autism," a comprehensive guide that sheds light on understanding and managing autism spectrum disorders.

One of Mr. Rajeev Bhatt's notable achievements is his role in tutoring Indian Dyslexic students to achieve top academic honors, breaking down barriers and stereotypes associated with learning differences. His impactful presence extends beyond national boundaries, as he has graced international platforms, captivating audiences with his profound insights into learning disabilities and mental health.

Mr. Rajeev Bhatt's multifaceted expertise encompasses psychology, education, and advocacy, making him a true trailblazer in fostering inclusive learning environments and nurturing the potential of every individual. His legacy continues to inspire educators, professionals, and families worldwide, championing the cause of inclusivity, empathy, and empowerment.

It has been more than two decades that we have been working relentlessly for our CHILDREN WITH DYSLEXIA and Other Disabilities with a well supported expert team consisting of special educators, Occupational therapists, Speech therapists, Behavior therapists, Counselling Psychologists, and Career Counsellors.
